AlbumsToBuy

AlbumsToBuy is one of my older, but favourite projects. It is a webshop for the fictional company "AlbumsToBuy". This company sells Albums in the form of either cd's or lp's.

Customers can browse the wide variety of different music albums, and look into the details, like which tracks are on the album.
If the customer wants the album they can order it, After which the customer gets an email confirming their purchase, and the stock is updated.

It is also possible to log in as an administrator, which gives you extra pages. On these pages you can update the albums, their tracks, the customers, their orders and more!

This is one of the biggest projects i have ever created, and it will always be one of the projects i am most proudest of.
