PulseRun

PulseRun is a small python game. It makes use of pygame, and is functional.
You control a ship's movements, and have to try to dodge the (targeted) attacks of the enemy ship. Your main goal is to survive as long as possible.

But you are not defenseless: during your run you can try to collect the batteries that appear at random, and increase your battery-count. And with a tab on the space bar, 4 pulses get shot out of the ship.
If one of the pulses hits the enemy, they are stunned for 5 seconds and won't do anything.

How long can you survive?
